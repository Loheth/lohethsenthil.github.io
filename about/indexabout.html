I actually started out more focused on front-end development, but as I gained experience, I expanded into back-end development, using tools like Databricks and Python to handle data and systems more effectively.

As a developer, I love crafting applications with a focus on precision and prototyping. The idea that a digital product is a living entity that can adapt and evolve fascinates me.

Beyond my technical pursuits, I enjoy being active in all forms, exploring new sports, breaking personal records and talking about my next meal.
